Hi, I'm Sushmita Bishwas, a Fashion Designer based in Noida with over 3 years of experience transforming ideas into thoughtfully crafted apparel. My work is driven by a passion for design that balances creativity, functionality, and market relevance.

Over the years, I have been involved in every stage of the design journey — from fabric selection to garment construction, sampling, and production coordination. I enjoy creating designs that not only reflect current fashion trends but also tell a story and connect with the people who wear them.

"I believe great fashion lies in the details. Whether developing a new collection or refining a single garment, I bring a strong eye for aesthetics, a deep understanding of silhouettes and textiles, and a commitment to quality craftsmanship."

As a designer, I am constantly inspired by evolving trends, culture, and everyday experiences. My goal is to create fashion that feels modern, purposeful, and enduring — while continuing to grow, learn, and contribute to the ever-changing world of design.
